# SEO Handover: Dominicating Google for "Calcera Global"

To ensure your site appears immediately when someone searches for your brand name, you must complete these three essential steps. Technical SEO is the engine, but these steps are the fuel.

## 1. Submit to Google Search Console (GSC)
Google needs to be told that your site exists.
1.  Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/about).
2.  Add your domain (`calcera.global`).
3.  Go to **Sitemaps** in the left menu.
4.  Enter `sitemap.xml` and click **Submit**.
5.  Use the **URL Inspection** tool (top search bar) for `https://calcera.global/` and click **Request Indexing**.

## 2. Setup Google Business Profile (GBP)
This is how you get the professional card on the right side of the search results.
1.  Go to [Google Business Profile](https://www.google.com/business/).
2.  Create a profile for **Calcera Global**.
3.  Add your location (**Shangri-La Hotel, Colombo-02**), phone number, and website.
4.  Verify your business (Google may send a postcard or call you).

## 3. Brand Authority Checklist
Google ranks "Calcera Global" higher when it sees the name across the web.
- [ ] **LinkedIn**: Ensure the company page name matches exactly.
- [ ] **Instagram**: Link back to the site in your bio.
- [ ] **Consistency**: Use the exact phrase "Calcera Global" in the first 100 words of any press release or post.

---
*Note: Once you submit to Search Console, it usually takes 24-72 hours for Google to update its index.*
